Need Help?

Welcome to the DryVibe support page. Here you’ll find answers to common questions, tips for troubleshooting, and ways to get in touch with our team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why didn’t my reminder fire? Make sure notifications are enabled for DryVibe in your device settings and that you’ve selected a valid reminder time.
  • Can I change my mocktail preferences? Yes! Tap the My Bar tab in the app to update your available ingredients at any time.
  • Does DryVibe work offline? All recipes are stored locally. You can browse and mix even without an internet connection.
  • How do I reset all settings? Go to Settings → Notification Settings → Toggle off then on to reinitialize notifications. For a full app reset, uninstall and reinstall the app.
